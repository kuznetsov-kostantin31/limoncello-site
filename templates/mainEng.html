Limoncello Philosophy

Limoncello restaurant is a place where warm family atmosphere, highest quality products and chef Alexander Bobryakov's skill are combined. Enjoy the taste of real Italy surrounded by nature 15 minutes from the city

Respecting traditions

We are proud to use an authentic Italian wood-fired Morello Forni oven to cook every pizza. A true symbol of Italian culinary tradition combined with the skill of our pizzaiolos helps to create the unique aroma and bright taste of pizza with genuine Italian quality

Vacation in comfort

Limoncello is located on the territory of the hotel and restaurant complex Aurora Park Hotel, surrounded by a picturesque park area, which is ideal for family outings. For our young guests we offer a spacious playroom and two playgrounds, and for relaxing on warm summer days we have a cozy outdoor terrace
